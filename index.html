Duarte da Costa is a communication and graphic designer. Throughout his academic career, and although initially in a candid way, he began to take an interest in the functionality and symbolism of images on a layout, not only in an attempt to try to understand the functional unemployment that tends to be attributed to them, but also to try to praise and privilege their use in tasks that were once ontological to design. Duarte da Costa's work seeks to communicate messages in which images are the preferred element for experimentation and use. ....................................
